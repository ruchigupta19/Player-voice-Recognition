Voice Control with web speech API and Speech speechSynthesis API

    COMMANDS APPLICATION
    PLAY or START Starts the video
    STOP or PAUSE Stops playing the video
    NEXT Jumps to next video
    PREVIOUS Goes back to the previous video
    MUTE Turns off the volume
    UNMUTE Turns on the volume
    JUMP TO N Seeks the video to n seconds
    DECREASE VOLUME or VOLUME DOWN Decreases volume
    INCREASE VOLUME or VOLUME UP Increases volume
    SHARE or SHARE MY VIDEO or SHARE VIDEO Displays share video screen
    SHARE VIDEO ON FACEBOOK|TWITTER|GOOGLE PLUS|LINKEDIN|PINTEREST|TUMBLR or JUST SAY "FACEBOOK" ETC Opens respective social media website (Works only if share screen is open)
    CLOSE Closes share video screen
